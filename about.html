Pakistan - ASEAN Trade Expo 2023

General Information
1

The Embassy of Pakistan in Jakarta Proudly Presents the First Pakistan – ASEAN Trade Development Conference & Single Country Exhibition (PTDCE) 2023 At Jakarta Convention Center, 18-20 January 2023. Right behind the trade development promo event initiative is the fact that Regional Comprehensive Economic Partnership (RCEP) carried huge economic potential where the paradigm is shifting from geo-politics to geo-economics where Pakistan would enable the country’s economy to reach milestones. The regional integration of ASEAN countries is essential for the economic prosperity of the ASEAN region as well as for global economic linkages and connectivity.

PTEDC 2023 aims to support ASEAN and Pakistan in promoting trade, economic, and investment relations through various means, including awareness raising and encouraging business-to-business contacts well as government-to-government. Bearing the big theme of “Trade with Pakistan, Enhance Global Value Chain”, all the 85 Exhibitors from Pakistan are showing off their latest export products for trade and business partnership in Indonesia and ASEAN region. Each exhibitors is represented by the decision-making level officers.
