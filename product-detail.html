Tolak Angin

Sido Muncul • East Java

First formulated in 1930, Tolak Angin is a standardised herbal remedy for cold/fever symptoms (masuk angin) that contains ginger, mint, fennel, screw tree, clove leaves, and honey. Tolak Angin has passed pre-clinical trials, is proven to improve the immune system, and is safe for long-term consumption. Tolak Angin is a Standardised Herbal Remedy (OHT) manufactured in a factory that meets standards in GMP (Good Manufacturing Product), ISO (International Organization of Standardization), dan HACCP (Hazard Analysis Critical Control Point).

Tolak Angin has passed sub-chronic toxicity tests and efficacy tests. Consuming 2 sachets of Tolak Angin for 7 days is proven to help maintain general wellness. Also available Tolak Angin Candy, Tolak Angin Care, and Balsam Tolak Angin.
