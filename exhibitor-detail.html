PT Industri Jamu dan Farmasi Sido Muncul Tbk. was established in 1940 and has produced more than 250 product types of traditional or herbal medicines, supplements, beverages, and confectioneries. In our processes, we use the latest production equipment and have adopted the following standards:

Traditional Medicines GMP, ISO 9001:2015 Quality Management Systems, ISO 14001:2015 Environmental Management Systems, ISO 22000:2009 Food Safety Management Systems, HACCP and Halal Assurance System.

Our quality control process enhance our products safety and quality. Our flagship products are Tolak Angin and KukuBima ENER-G!. We have been export our products to several countries in Southeast Asia, America, and Africa
